USEFULL COMMANDS

  • /kit [gives you kits]
  • /rtp [teleports you to a random spot in the world]
  • /tpa (player) [sends a tp request to the player u selected]
  • /spawn [teleports you to spawn]
  • /sethome (home name) [creates a home tp]
  • /home (home name) [tps you to the selected home]
